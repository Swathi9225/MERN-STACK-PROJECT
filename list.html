Recipe Description

Tapenadeis a quick and tastyapread made for olives,garlic,and a splash of lemon.
            It's super easy to prepare and pack a bold,savory flavor.That goes perfectly with bread, crackers, or veggies.

Instructions :

1. First take a jar.
2. Add olives, garlic, capers, Lemon Juice
3. Blend roughly (don't make it too smooth).
4. Add Olive oil slowly and blend again.
5. Taste and add salt & Pepper.
6. sprinkel some goat milk or normal cheese.
7. Serve on bread or use as dip.
