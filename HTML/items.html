ABOUT ITEMS

The Items

Chicken, fish, tabbouleh, and fries make for a diverse and
globally-inspired meal that expertly balances protein, fresh vegetables,
and satisfying starches. The meal could feature grilled or fried chicken and fish,
perhaps served with a light lemon drizzle, complementing their savory and lean profiles.

The chicken

Chicken fry is a popular, versatile dish with many global and regional variations,
ranging from Indian-style spicy preparations (often a dry stir-fry or deep-fried starter)
to classic Southern-style buttermilk fried chicken. Key steps generally involve marinating the chicken in seasonings,
sometimes with a coating or batter, and then frying until cooked through and crispy.

The Fish

Fish recipes offer a wide range of delicious and healthy meal options,
from quick pan-fries to rich curries and simple baked dishes.
The key to a great fish recipe often lies in a good marinade and not overcooking the fish,
which tends to cook quickly.

The tabouleh

Tabouleh (also spelled tabouli) is a fresh, vibrant Middle Eastern herb salad
where finely chopped parsley is the main ingredient, complemented by fresh mint, tomatoes, onion,
and a small amount of bulgur wheat.The salad is dressed simply with extra-virgin olive oil and fresh lemon juice

The Fries

French fries are popular deep-fried potato strips, crispy outside and soft inside,
seasoned with salt, and served as a side or snack with various sauces like ketchup or mayo;
they come in many cuts (shoestring, waffle, curly)
